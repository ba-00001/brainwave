Storyboard

Storyboard for Sarah’s Scenario

Step 1: Sarah logs into BrainWave Diagnostics and sees an easy-to-follow prompt for uploading brain scans.

Step 2: She selects files from her device (qEEG and SPECT) and uploads them; a status bar confirms progress.

Step 3: The platform processes the data, showing a brief loading animation.

Step 4: A summary report appears with an ADHD likelihood score and markers, like the theta/beta ratio, presented in simple visuals.

Step 5: She downloads the report and sees a prompt to consult with her healthcare provider, leaving her reassured and informed.

Storyboard for Dr. Perez’s Scenario

Step 1: Dr. Perez logs into BrainWave Diagnostics, accessing a dashboard with recent cases.

Step 2: He selects a patient case and uploads fMRI and qEEG scans.

Step 3: The system processes the scans, showing a brief loading animation.

Step 4: The detailed report appears, displaying key metrics like dopamine levels and connectivity.

Step 5: Dr. Perez downloads a simplified version of the report for parent discussions and exports data for his records.
