Scenarios

Scenario: Parent (Sarah Thompson)

Sarah, a caring parent, has observed patterns of inattention and hyperactivity in her child and, after consultations, decides to use BrainWave Diagnostics for additional insights.

Initial Login and Guidance: Sarah logs into BrainWave Diagnostics, where the landing page offers a friendly, guided walkthrough. A prominent “Upload Brain Scans” button with a step-by-step guide reassures her on the steps needed.

Uploading Brain Scans: Sarah chooses to upload her child’s SPECT and qEEG scan files. The platform uses visual aids, providing icons for each file type and giving clear feedback on successful uploads.

Analysis and Report Generation: The system processes the scans and presents a summary within minutes. The report highlights potential markers of ADHD, including brainwave irregularities, and simplifies technical terms to help her understand the report’s implications.

Reviewing Recommendations: A final screen suggests consulting with a healthcare provider to discuss the results further. Sarah can download the report to bring to her child’s next doctor’s appointment and appreciates the clear call to action.

Scenario: Healthcare Professional (Dr. David Perez)

Dr. Perez, a pediatric neurologist, regularly uses BrainWave Diagnostics in his clinical practice to support ADHD diagnoses, especially in complex cases.

Dashboard Overview and Patient Selection: Dr. Perez logs into the platform and accesses his personalized dashboard, displaying a list of recent patient cases. He selects a new patient case and is directed to a detailed upload interface for different scan types.

Uploading and Comparing Scan Data: He uploads fMRI and qEEG scans. BrainWave Diagnostics recognizes the file formats and shows a processing notification with estimated time for results, allowing Dr. Perez to multitask as he waits.

Detailed Analysis Report: The system produces an in-depth report showing data on dopamine transporter binding, functional connectivity, and theta/beta ratio. Key metrics are highlighted with interpretation guides, helping Dr. Perez explain findings to parents with confidence.

Diagnostic Support and Consultation Tools: A “Consultation Summary” feature enables Dr. Perez to download a simplified version of the report for parents, enhancing their understanding. He can also export data for integration into his electronic health record (EHR) system, streamlining his practice.
